Nature Graphics

Nature Graphics is a graphic design firm specializing in the planning, writing, design, fabrication and installation of interpretive displays and wayfinding signage. Established in 2003, we are experienced enough to handle the largest of wayside exhibit projects yet eager enough to welcome even the smallest projects.

The Owner

Lea Beazley understands first-hand the importance of effective interpretive displays in parks. Before starting her own company, she worked as a park ranger and district interpretation & education specialist for North Carolina State Parks. She saw how poorly designed interpretive displays and wayfinding signage detract from the visitor experience while well-designed displays and signage enhance it.

-Lea Beazley

Our Mission

To create interpretive displays, wayfinding signage, and park information displays that are helpful, engaging, attractive, ADA-accessible, durable and affordable while giving all of our clients the personal attention they deserve.
